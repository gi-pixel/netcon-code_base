about us

Engineering the networks that power business

NETCON was founded to bring enterprise‑grade networking and security to organizations that demand reliability without complexity.

From day one, we've been different

NETCON started in 2012 when three network engineers saw the same problem: businesses were forced to choose between expensive legacy vendors or underpowered consumer gear. There was no middle ground for organizations that needed professional, scalable infrastructure without the six‑figure consulting fees.

We built NETCON to be that middle ground deep technical expertise, vendor‑agnostic advice, and transparent pricing. Today, we manage over 500 networks across 23 countries, but our approach hasn't changed: listen first, design second, and always put long‑term resilience over short‑term fixes.
